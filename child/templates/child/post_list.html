Children Welfare Committee

Children Welfare Committee (CWC) is a body of the State Government under the Juvenile Justice Act to look after cases of children in need of care and protection.

CWC functions like a court and conducts regular visits to district’s CCIs.At least 3 members of CWC must be present when disposing a case.

The majority opinion prevails. The chairperson carries the deciding vote in case of no majority. The maximum limit for CWCs to dispose any pending case is 4 months.

If a child is abandoned, orphaned or surrendered, CWC can declare the child legally free for adoption.
